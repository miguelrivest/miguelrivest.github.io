About Us

PreciseEdu was founded by a group of former students driven by a common goal – transforming the way students access accurate resources for their assignments and homework. As former students ourselves, we understand the importance of reliable information when your education is in question.

Our Team

Matt

Matt is a fellow computer science graduate that believed in the mission of bringing a quality education to every person trying to access it online with their school. He has played an integral part in the development of our product and has brought forth many new features.
