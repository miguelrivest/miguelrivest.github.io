What is PreciseEdu?

Here at PreciseEdu, our mission is to provide a quality environment that benefits students and teachers alike, at an affordable price. As former students ourselves, we wanted to provide every school with the opportunity to have an archive of answered questions at their disposal, without the anxiety that comes with finding information online that might be applicable to your course, no matter the time.

What would this look like for my school?

First, if you're a teacher, it looks like having more time doing the million other things you have to do by not having to answer common questions in your office hours or 1-1 sessions. As a student, this means not having to attend so many 1-1 sessions, saving you time to do... well, whatever else you would rather be doing.
